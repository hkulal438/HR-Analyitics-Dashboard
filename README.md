HR Analytics Dashboard

This HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive, interactive view of employee attrition and workforce metrics to support data-driven decision-making in Human Resources. It summarizes key indicators such as total employee count, attrition numbers, attrition rate, average age, average salary, and average years of service. The dashboard includes visual breakdowns of attrition across multiple dimensions such as education field, age group, gender, salary range, years at the company, and job role. These insights help HR managers quickly identify turnover patterns, understand the factors influencing attrition, and strengthen employee retention strategies.

**Features**
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
Displays total employees, attrition count, attrition rate, average age, average salary, and tenure.

Attrition Analysis:
Breakdown by:
Education field
Age groups
Gender
Salary ranges
Years at company
Job role

**Interactive Visuals:**
Includes bar charts, donut charts, line graphs, and comparison visuals for better interpretation.
**User-Friendly Layout:**
Clean, organized design for easy navigation and quick insights.
Tools & Technologies Used
Power BI for dashboard development
Data Modeling using DAX measures and calculated columns
Interactive Visuals to represent employee behavior patterns
Purpose of the Dashboard

**The main goal of this dashboard is to help HR teams:**

Identify high-risk categories where attrition is more common
Understand demographic and salary-based trends
Analyze departmental and job role turnover
Build effective employee retention strategies
Support leadership with data-backed decisions
